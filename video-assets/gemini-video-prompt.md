# MatchFit Marketing Video — Gemini Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create an upbeat, professional marketing video for **MatchFit** — a soccer fitness platform that connects coaches with athletes. The video should feel energetic and modern, like a SaaS product launch video.

The video is ONE continuous piece with THREE acts. Each act features a different person who introduces themselves before their segment begins. The viewer should always know whose perspective they're seeing.

Include an animated intro with the MatchFit logo (green lightning bolt + "MatchFit" text, green brand color #16a34a).

## Brand
- **Name**: MatchFit
- **Tagline**: "Train Like A Pro. Play Like A Champion."
- **Brand Color**: Green (#16a34a / #22c55e)
- **Logo**: Lightning bolt ⚡ + "MatchFit"
- **URL**: matchfit.stproperties.com

## Style Notes
- Upbeat, energetic background music (think SaaS product launch)
- Smooth transitions between screenshots (zoom, slide, fade)
- Ken Burns effect on screenshots (subtle pan/zoom to add motion)
- Text overlays should be clean, modern, white on dark semi-transparent bars
- Each act should have a distinct color accent:
  - Act 1 (Coach): Green (#16a34a)
  - Act 2 (Player): Blue (#0ea5e9)
  - Act 3 (Review): Purple (#7c3aed)
- Total runtime: ~2 minutes
